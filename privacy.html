Morse Watcher Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

Camera Access

Morse Watcher requests camera access to detect flashing light patterns and decode Morse code timing. Camera access is used only while the app is actively monitoring a selected light source.

Data Handling

Morse Watcher does not store photos, videos, or camera recordings. The app does not transmit camera data, decoded output, or any personal data to external servers.

All signal analysis and decoding are performed on-device. No data leaves your device through Morse Watcher.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact support@morsewatcher.com.
