Privacy Policy

Morse Watcher Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

Scope

This policy covers both the Morse Watcher iOS app and the Morse Watcher website.

iOS App Privacy

Morse Watcher requests camera access only to detect flashing light patterns and decode Morse code timing. Camera access is used only while the app is actively monitoring a selected light source.

Morse Watcher does not collect personal information, does not require account creation, and does not use advertising identifiers.

Morse Watcher does not store photos, videos, or camera recordings. The app does not transmit camera data, decoded output, or any personal data to external servers.

All signal analysis and decoding are performed on-device. No data leaves your device through Morse Watcher.

Website Privacy

The Morse Watcher website is a public informational site. We do not provide user accounts, and we do not intentionally collect personal information through the site.

We do not use advertising trackers, behavioral profiling, or social media tracking pixels on this site.

Like most websites, basic technical request data (such as IP address, browser details, and access time) may be processed by hosting, DNS, and content-delivery providers to serve the site and maintain security.

This site also loads typography from Google Fonts, which may involve requests from your browser to Google servers for font files.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal information. We do not share app camera data with third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact support@morsewatcher.com.
