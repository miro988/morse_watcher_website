App Introduction

Three moments that explain how Morse Watcher works.

Tap screen to start

Start the detector view, choose the light you want to watch, and lock onto the right signal source while the app automatically calibrates timing and detection settings.

Decode while tracking

Once calibration is complete, Morse Watcher tracks the light in real time and converts the flashes into readable output.

Send with Morse sender

The sender screen lets you compose a message, convert it into Morse timing, and transmit it with a controlled flashlight pulse.

Offline Communication

No signal? No internet? Use Morse Watcher to communicate.

When phone signal drops and internet is unavailable, Morse Watcher helps you send and receive simple messages using flashlight pulses and camera detection.

Two phones on fixed ground can exchange Morse patterns with stable framing, controlled flashing, and visible decode feedback.
